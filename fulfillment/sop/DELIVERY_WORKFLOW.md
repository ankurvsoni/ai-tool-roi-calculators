# Delivery Workflow (No-Scam SOP)

## Rule
Do not accept payment unless the promised deliverable is ready and sendable.

## Intake -> Delivery
1. Receive form submission
2. Qualify scope and confirm tier
3. Send payment link + delivery timeline
4. Deliver assets by SLA
5. Ask for confirmation + testimonial request

## SLA
- Toolkit: instant download after payment
- Starter Audit: 48 hours
- Advanced Audit: 3–5 business days

## Quality Gate
- Every output must include assumptions
- Every recommendation must include risk notes
- Include one-page executive summary
