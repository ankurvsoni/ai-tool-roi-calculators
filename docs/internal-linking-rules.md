# Internal Linking Rules

1. Every calculator links to:
   - 2 relevant comparisons
   - 1 guide
   - 1 template
2. Every comparison links to:
   - 2 calculators and 1 guide
3. Every guide links to:
   - 3 calculators and 2 comparisons
4. Use contextual anchors (not "click here")
5. Max 6-8 internal links per page body
