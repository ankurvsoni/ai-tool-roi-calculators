# Welcome to the AI ROI Toolkit

This toolkit is built for non-technical users.

## What you can do in 15 minutes
1. Estimate if an AI tool is worth paying for.
2. See your likely monthly savings.
3. Build a simple recommendation for your team.

## Quick Path
- Open **01-ROI-CALCULATOR.csv** in Google Sheets
- Fill yellow input cells (team size, tool cost, hours saved)
- Review conservative / base / aggressive scenarios
- Use **05-DECISION-MEMO-TEMPLATE.md** to summarize your decision

## Need help?
Use the examples in **02-EXAMPLE-SCENARIOS.csv** first, then replace with your own numbers.
