# Opportunity Plan — AI Tool Cost & ROI Calculators

- **Niche:** AI software buying decisions with calculator-first content (cost, ROI, break-even)
- **Primary audience:** founders, solo creators, freelancers, small marketing teams
- **Secondary audience:** ops managers and agency owners comparing AI stack spend vs output

## 3) Competitors + Differentiation
Competitors:
- aitools.fyi (tool directories)
- g2 category pages (broad, non-calculator)
- vendor pricing pages (single-tool view)
- generic ROI template blogs
- Capterra comparison pages

Differentiation:
- calculator-native UX (not just static comparison)
- scenario-based templates by role/use-case
- transparent formulas + assumptions toggle
- "sensitivity view" (best/base/worst case)
- actionable recommendation output (keep/replace/test)

## 4) Monetization Plan

## 7) Risks + Assumptions
Assumptions:
- users need decision support beyond vendor pricing pages
- calculator pages can rank for high-intent keywords
- formula transparency increases trust and conversions

Risks:
- pricing data drift
- over-simplified assumptions reduce credibility
- low differentiation if calculators are too generic

Mitigations:
- monthly price update job
- show assumptions/editable variables
- include scenario sensitivity + confidence notes
