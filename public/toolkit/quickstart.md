# AI ROI Toolkit Quickstart (15 minutes)

1. Open `roi-calculator-template.csv` in Google Sheets.
2. Enter your real monthly spend, team size, hourly rate, and hours saved.
3. Duplicate the sheet for conservative/base/aggressive scenarios.
4. Use `assumptions-library.csv` to sanity-check your numbers.
5. Fill `decision-memo-template.md` to present your recommendation.
6. Track outcomes weekly in `kpi-tracker-template.csv`.
